SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

How this project supports sustainable development goals

🌍

Sustainable Development Goal 8

Decent Work and Economic Growth

🎯 Target 8.5: Achieve full and productive employment

This AI-powered resume screening system helps organizations efficiently identify qualified candidates, reducing time-to-hire and improving the quality of hires. By automating the initial screening process, recruiters can focus on meaningful interactions with candidates.

💼 Target 8.6: Substantially reduce the proportion of youth not in employment

The system provides objective, skills-based evaluation that reduces bias in recruitment. This helps young professionals and entry-level candidates get fair consideration based on their actual skills and potential rather than subjective factors.

⚡ Target 8.3: Promote development-oriented policies

By leveraging AI and cloud technologies (AWS Comprehend, S3), this project demonstrates how technology can be used to create more efficient and inclusive hiring processes, supporting economic growth through better talent matching.

🤝 Target 8.8: Protect labour rights and promote safe working environments

The system's transparent scoring and recommendation process provides clear feedback to recruiters, helping ensure fair and consistent evaluation practices across all candidates.

📊 Project Impact

  • Efficiency: Reduces resume screening time by up to 80%
  • Objectivity: Provides data-driven, unbiased candidate evaluation
  • Accessibility: Makes job opportunities more accessible through fair screening
  • Innovation: Demonstrates AI application for social good
  • Scalability: Can be deployed globally to support diverse hiring needs
